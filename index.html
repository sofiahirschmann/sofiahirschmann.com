About

I’m a PhD student in Computer Science at Northeastern University. My work sits at the intersection of cognitive science, human-AI interaction, and computational modeling, with a focus on how humans and intelligent systems coordinate, adapt, and make decisions together.

I’m especially interested in Theory of Mind, adaptive systems, human-AI collaboration, and decision-making under uncertainty. More broadly, I’m motivated by questions about how technology can understand people more meaningfully, support human agency, and become a better collaborator in complex social and decision-making contexts.
